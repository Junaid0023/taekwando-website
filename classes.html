Our Taekwondo Classes

1. Beginner's Class

Description: This class is designed for individuals who are new to Taekwondo. You'll learn the basics of martial arts, including stances, basic movements, and self-discipline. Perfect for those who are just starting their martial arts journey!

Target Audience: Anyone aged 6 and up with little to no prior experience.

Class Duration: 1 hour

Instructor: Master Kim

2. Intermediate Class

Description: For those who have a basic understanding of Taekwondo and are ready to enhance their skills. This class builds upon your foundational knowledge and introduces more complex techniques, including advanced footwork, combinations, and self-defense techniques.

Target Audience: Students who have completed the Beginner's Class or have previous martial arts experience.

Class Duration: 1 hour

Instructor: Grandmaster Lee

3. Advanced Class

Description: The Advanced class is for Taekwondo practitioners who want to push their limits. Here, you’ll focus on perfection, speed, power, and flexibility. Advanced techniques, sparring, and forms (poomsae) are covered extensively.

Target Audience: Black belts and high-level practitioners.

Class Duration: 1.5 hours

Instructor: Grandmaster Lee

4. Kids’ Taekwondo

Description: Taekwondo is an excellent way for kids to develop confidence, focus, and respect. Our Kids’ Taekwondo classes teach martial arts in a fun, engaging, and structured environment that emphasizes discipline, respect, and self-improvement.

Target Audience: Children aged 6-12.

Class Duration: 45 minutes

Instructor: Instructor Park

5. Taekwondo for Fitness

Description: This class is focused on improving physical fitness using Taekwondo techniques. It’s a combination of strength training, endurance, and flexibility exercises, perfect for individuals looking to stay fit while learning self-defense skills.

Target Audience: Anyone looking for a fun and challenging workout.

Class Duration: 1 hour

Instructor: Coach Chang

6. Sparring Class

Description: Sparring is an essential aspect of Taekwondo, where students can practice their techniques in a controlled, competitive environment. This class focuses on improving your sparring skills, including strategy, reflexes, and sportsmanship.

Target Audience: Intermediate and advanced students who are interested in competitive Taekwondo or improving their fighting skills.

Class Duration: 1 hour

Instructor: Master Choi

7. Self-Defense Class

Description: In this class, you’ll learn practical self-defense techniques based on Taekwondo principles. The focus is on real-world situations where you might need to protect yourself, including basic escapes, strikes, and defense strategies.

Target Audience: Anyone looking to feel more confident in their ability to defend themselves.

Class Duration: 1 hour

Instructor: Instructor Park

8. Family Class

Description: A fun, family-friendly class where parents and kids can train together! This class is designed for families who want to bond while learning Taekwondo, enhancing physical fitness, discipline, and teamwork.

Target Audience: Families with children (age 5+).

Class Duration: 1 hour

Instructor: Master Kim
